How Solar Panels Work

Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity through the photovoltaic effect. When photons from sunlight hit the silicon cells in a solar panel, they knock electrons loose from their atoms. This flow of electrons creates an electrical current that can be captured and used to power your home or business.
